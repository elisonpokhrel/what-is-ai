Artificial Intelligence (AI)

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the science of making machines that can think and learn like humans. These systems can process information, make decisions, and adapt based on their experiences.

Examples of AI in action:

  • Chatbots like ChatGPT, Gemini, Copilot, Meta AI, etc.
  • Virtual assistants like Siri, Alexa, and Google Assistant
  • Recommendation engines on YouTube, Netflix, or Spotify
  • Facial recognition in security systems
  • Spam detection in emails
