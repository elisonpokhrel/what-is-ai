✅ Advantages of AI

Good things AI can do better than humans in some cases:

  • Works fast and doesn’t need breaks
  • Never forgets information
  • Can handle big problems or lots of data
  • Works in dangerous places (like space or deep oceans)
  • Doesn’t get tired, bored, or emotional
